Welcome to AmaZING!

AmaZING! is the home of world class throwing sticks, used in stick throwing tournaments across 125 countries. With over 200 years of stick production under its belt, AmaZING! is more than the finest shop to purchase your next stick, it's the gold (or shall we say brown) standard.

Founded with the principles of distance throwing coupled with a striking balance between speed and our trademark satisfying auditory feedback mechanism (i.e. the 'ZING'), we have continued to produce by hand a product unparalleled for its craftsmanship and general awesomeness.

Please peruse the info on our site to learn more about stick purchasing, to see a full product list, or to contact us.
